Basta ambot

Night Walk

As the moon glistens from above

You and I hold hands as we walk through the pavement

Talking about the stuff that we dont want the world to know

Being afraid of what the world would thinks about us if they knew

Hearing the stories of others frightens our bones

Society judging a vast majority us, we dont want to know

We want to be seen and heard by others

But we continue to hide ourselves, we are scared...
